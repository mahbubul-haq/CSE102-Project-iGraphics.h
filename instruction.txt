Start play button to play.
Then select resume or any level to play. You are in the game.
Initially you have 2 lifes. Scores are on the left top corner of the screen.
Do not let the ball fall down. Use the red stick to save the ball from falling.
To move the stick you can use either left-right key in the keyboard or
you can use mouse but left button of mouse should be pressed all the time.
To pause the game press 'p', to resume press 'r', to quit press 'q'
while quitting you can save the current state of the game.
After one life is lost you need to press 'r' to resume. Enjoy!
